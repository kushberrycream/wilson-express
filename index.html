Services

Overnight Deliveries / Collections

As part of the APC Overnight Network we are able to service the majority of the UK, excluding the Highlands of Scotland and British Isles, with a 24Hour Overnight Service. We deliver 6 Days of the week, Monday to Saturday, boasting an impressive 99% on time Delivery Rate.

Sameday Deliveries

Need something delivered quickly? To Large for Overnight Delivery? We can get a dedicated vehicle to you the very same day and delivered to the delivery point you choose! Call our Customer Services team to find out if our Sameday service is for you

International Deliveries / Collections

We have access to all the major Couriers such as UPS, DHL and TNT giving our customers the benefit of multiple services to fit their budgets and time limits. We can reach the Majority of the world in 1-2 days meaning all your time critical deliveries will get their on time.

Full Track and Trace

All parcels leaving our depot leave with full tracking! With APC PinPoint our customers will be able to view the drivers current location all the way up to the delivery. Internationals are all sent fully tracked then services such as Follow My Delivery by UPS will provide tracking to the reciever.

Liquid, Fragile, Premium Services

Need something delivered in the Morning? No problem we have 3 timed services, pre-9, pre-10 and pre-12 Deliveries Esuring your delivery is there on time. We specialise in Liquid deliveries such as Wine, working with multiple Wine producers we handle hundreds of wine boxes daily. We will also take great care of any fragile items manually handling every item.
